THE COW

AN ESSAY ABOUT COW

The cow, a domesticated bovine species, holds a central place in agriculture and human society. Known primarily for its milk production, cows also provide meat, leather, and other valuable resources.With their gentle demeanor and iconic appearance, they symbolize pastoral life and have become ingrained in cultural traditions worldwide. Beyond their economic importance, cows contribute to ecological balance through grazing and nutrient cycling.
